Recent Albums I've Been Into

Marilyn Manson (We Are Chaos)

"We Are Chaos" is the most recent album release from the group Marilyn Manson. Many people aren't fans of his work but I enjoy it as I beleive their is true creativity behind his work!

Two Door Cinema Club (Tourist History)

"Tourist History" is in my opinion one of Two Door Cinema Clubs classic and greatest albums to date. I would even go as far as to say its the poster child of the genre "Alternative".

Daft Punk "Random Access Memories"

"Random Access Memoreis" was one of the more essential albums that really go me into music at a young age. I am still a very large fan of the cult group, Daft Punk.
